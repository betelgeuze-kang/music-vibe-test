ABOUT THIS LISTENING NOTEBOOK

We do not diagnose a listener.
We keep the scenes music brings them back to.

My Music Vibe is a small, account-free listening notebook. Ten choices become six directions, five songs for the room you are in, and—if you invite someone—a sequence with space for two different ears.

Operated by betelgeuze-kang · Last reviewed July 13, 2026

What the service does

  • Places eight original, sample-free listening clips beside everyday scenes.
  • Writes the ten choices as six directions rather than a permanent personality label.
  • Chooses five songs in a deliberate order: three close to the note, one side road, and one from a little farther away.
  • Builds a shared list from two anonymous notes without pretending to know what either person will love.
  • Keeps profile history, feedback, and Weekly Vibe in the browser unless the user exports it.

What the service does not do

  • It is not a medical, psychological, or personality diagnosis.
  • It does not copy commercial audio files, lyrics, or album artwork onto this server.
  • It does not read a Spotify, YouTube, or Apple Music account or external listening history.
  • It does not imply endorsement or affiliation with a platform, artist, or label.
  • It does not make a result or recommendation conditional on an advertisement click.

How a recommendation is made

Each candidate is read through six musical directions, the moment selected for today, variety across artist, region, and era, and the listener’s bounded feedback. A few button presses cannot overwrite the original note. The short explanations are written here; they do not quote lyrics or copy another review.

When a direct platform link has been reviewed, the interface says so. Otherwise the link opens a title-and-artist search rather than pretending to be exact.

Commercial operation

The domain has an authorized Google AdSense seller record, but advertising delivery is currently off. There is no live advertising script, slot, or advertising cookie in the current release. Before any experiment begins, the privacy choices, placement, consent mode, and invalid-traffic safeguards must pass a separate release gate.
